03 — Experience

Where I've worked

Two experiences, two regulated industries, one consistent focus: turning messy operational data into something people can actually use.

Decembre 2025 — June 2026 Tunis, Tunisia
Data Engineer
SIBTEL - Société Interbancaire de Télécompensation
Final-year internship (PFE)

SIBTEL is Tunisia's interbank teleclearing operator — every interbank payment in the country (check, wire transfer, direct debit) passes through its systems. Before this project, performance analysis relied entirely on manual queries against the production database, exported to Excel. I was brought in to design and build a complete analytics platform from scratch, with full ownership from architecture to deployment.

  • Built a validated geographic referential for bank agencies by merging three data sources (internal Oracle data, the Central Bank's public agency registry, and Tunisia Post reference data) into a single version of truth, via automated n8n workflows.
  • Designed a Kimball-style star-schema data warehouse on PostgreSQL/TimescaleDB with one fact table and five dimensions, executing two schema migrations while preserving surrogate key stability.
  • Automated the full ELT pipeline with Apache Airflow (6-step DAG) and dbt, including 40 automated data quality tests - cutting reporting time from 1–3 days to under 15 minutes.
  • Shipped a self-service BI portal in Metabase - six dashboards covering national overview, bank comparison, agency network, payment analytics, 360° bank views, and regional performance.
  • Built a full monitoring & observability stack (Prometheus, Grafana, Alertmanager, 3 exporters) with 8 alert rules covering pipeline health, data quality, database performance, and infrastructure.
  • Containerized the entire platform 11 services on Docker Compose, deployable with a single command.
